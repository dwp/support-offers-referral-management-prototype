WHP Minister

Overview

The Work and Health Programme (WHP) provides dedicated and flexible support for up to 15 months. It focuses on:

  • breaking down barriers to working
  • learning new skills
  • building confidence
  • increasing work experience

6 months of in-work support is also available.

It is voluntary to take part in the opportunity, unless the person has been unemployed for 24 months or more.

Eligibility

To be eligible they must be claiming Job Seekers Allowance (JSA).

They must also:

  • be disabled, as defined by the Equality Act 2010
  • be autistic
  • be over 18 years old in England, or 16 years old in Wales
  • have the right to work in the UK
  • not be in any paid work, including zero hours contracts
  • not currently be on the WHP
  • not have been on the WHP before
  • not be on another national opportunity

Suitability

To be suitable they must:

  • have committed to the goal of finding work in 12 months
  • need extra support to find work

To take part in this opportunity, they must meet all the eligibility and suitability criteria.
