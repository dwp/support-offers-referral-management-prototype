Universal Support - out of work -
Individual Placement and Support (IPS)

Overview

Universal Support helps people who face complex barriers to work. 

Individual Placement and Support (IPS) integrates with health and support services to deliver support as part of their normal health treatment.

Support is provided for 12 months. This can be extended by up to 6 months.

Eligibility

To be eligible they must be in one of the participant groups:

  • have a disability or a long-term health condition
  • be a veteran
  • be in a specified disadvantaged group

They must also:

  • not be in paid work
  • be aged 18 or over in England or aged 16 or over in Wales
  • have the right to work in the UK
  • have the right to live in the UK and be a resident of England or Wales
  • have entitlement to public funds
  • not currently be on another national contracted opportunity
  • be available to participate
