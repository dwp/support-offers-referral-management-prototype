Connect to Work - Individual Placement and Support (IPS) - Out of Work

Overview

Connect to Work IPS provides:

  • personalised support for people who are unemployed and want to work
  • vocational profiling and job matching which integrates with health and support services
  • support for up to 12 months, which can be extended by up to 6 months

Eligibility

Connect to Work supports people with disadvantages in the labour market who are:

  • disabled or have a long-term health condition as defined in the Equality Act 2010 or the Social Model of Disability

or

  • in a specified disadvantaged group

To be eligible they must:

  • not be doing any paid work and be available to start a suitable job
  • have the right to work in the UK
  • have the right to live in the UK and be a resident of England or Wales
  • have entitlement to public funds
  • not currently be on another DWP employment programme

They should also:

  • be aged 18 or over in England or aged 16 or over in Wales

Suitability

They must meet the suitability criteria, as set out in the Grant Guidance. Connect to Work IPS should be the most appropriate support for them, and they should be ready to engage with the programme.
