Restart

Overview

Restart provides personalised support for up to 12 months to help people get back into work. It focuses on:

  • developing skills to boost employability
  • helping break down barriers to working
! Warning Anyone who is eligible must take part in this opportunity or their benefits could be affected.

Eligibility

To be eligible they must have been claiming Job Seekers Allowance (JSA) for 9 months or longer

They must also:

  • be over 18 years old in England or over 16 years old in Wales
  • have been claiming Job Seekers Allowance (JSA) for 9 months or longer
  • have no sustained PAYE earnings
  • have the right to work in the UK
  • not currently be on Restart
  • not have been on Restart before
  • not currently be on another national opportunity
  • not be gainfully self-employed

Suitability

To be suitable they must:

  • not be waiting for a self-employed Gateway Intervention or a Gainfully Self-employed (GSE) decision
  • not have started a job
  • not be waiting to hear the outcome of a job interview or application
  • not be currently participating in a Sector-based Work Academy Programme (SWAP)
  • not be waiting to hear the outcome of a SWAP job application or guaranteed job interview
  • not have an open-ended sanction currently in place
  • not have significant barriers to engaging with work related activity of this intensity

To take part in this opportunity, they must meet all the eligibility and suitability criteria.
