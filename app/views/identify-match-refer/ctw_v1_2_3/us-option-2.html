Connect to Work - in work retention support - Supported Employment Quality Framework (SEQF)

Overview

  • personalised support for people who are at risk of losing work
  • SEQF provides vocational profiling and job matching for vulnerable job seekers
  • support is provided for 4 months and can be extended by 2 months

Eligibility

Connect to Work supports people who are:

  • disabled or have a long-term health condition
  • in a specified disadvantaged group

They must also:

  • be at risk of dropping out of work
  • have been employed continually for at least 3 months
  • be aged 18 or over in England or aged 16 or over in Wales
  • have the right to work in the UK
  • have the right to live in the UK and be a resident of England or Wales
  • have entitlement to public funds
  • not currently be on another national contracted opportunity
  • be available to participate
