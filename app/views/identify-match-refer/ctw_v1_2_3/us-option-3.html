Connect to Work - out of work -
Individual Placement and Support (IPS)

Overview

  • personalised support for people who are unemployed
  • IPS integrates with health and support services
  • support is provided for 12 months and can be extended by up to 6 months

Eligibility

Connect to Work supports people who are:

  • disabled or have a long-term health condition
  • in a specified disadvantaged group

They must also:

  • not be in paid work
  • be aged 18 or over in England or aged 16 or over in Wales
  • have the right to work in the UK
  • have the right to live in the UK and be a resident of England or Wales
  • have entitlement to public funds
  • not currently be on another national contracted opportunity
  • be available to participate
