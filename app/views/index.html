The vision

Support offers will connect people with knowledge, skills and opportunities that can improve their quality of life and financial resilience, and help them into appropriate work wherever possible.

{#

Strategy

Support Offers will take a person-centred approach: develop knowledge of a person’s circumstances to determine their needs and recommend actions tailored to their needs. Recommendations for individuals will reflect their complexity as a person; Support Offers will treat the whole person, not just the symptom of worklessness, to meet DWP objectives of quality of life and financial resilience in a balanced way.

Prototypes

Support offers covers 7 different work streams, which are interdependent. Our work so far has included 3 of those areas.

Currently, we are focused on 2 work streams in parallel with the work of the system architects. Those areas are identify, match and refer and referral management.

The team

Support Offers has one central team and 2 feature teams. The central team is responsible for both the overall direction and project architecture. The feature teams are focusing on one work stream each. Team Airedale is focused on identify, match and refer. Team Nidderdale is focused on referral management.
